Combining music and career

Jameela has always included music in her path in life. Here are a few YouTube video examples thought-out the years.

    • InTune & Premium Pitch

    Jameela is a Software Engineer in San Francisco California, where she is part of Internote, an inter-tech company acapella group that before the quarantine performed at Techapella.

    This song "Say You Won't Let Go” originally written by James Arthur, was performed virtually by LinkedIn, InTune and Premium Pitch.

    top

    • Iowa Agni at Gathe Raho

    When Jameela went for her masters’ program in Science in Education in Iowa she was the musical director of Iowa Agni.

    Iowa Agni is an acapella music group blending Indian music and English pop tunes. During that time they hosted Gathe Raho. Gathe Raho is one of the biggest academic Bollywood acapella competitions in the US.

    The song “Stronger/Challa” written by Kelly Clarkson was arranged by Jameela in both English and Urdu for this event.

    Jameela received her bachelor’s degree in vocal music from Northern Illinois University. Here she is performing "Habanera" from Carmen for her audition entrance.
