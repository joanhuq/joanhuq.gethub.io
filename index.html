Bio

I am a mother of 3 children and they are my life’s primary focus. They are major reasons for my life's choices. Each one of them has unique stories, personalities, and passions. In this site I am sharing only one part of their lives.

Jameela my oldest daughter since kindergarten has taken many paths to where she is now. She has made numerous, educational and career changes but one passion remained constant, her love for music. So if your passion is music but making your living in a different field. You especially will find her story very interesting.

Solaman my middle child, got married a few years back to a great girl named Sasha. What is unique is that this wedding took place in Pakistan. How it was a arrange marriage with unfamiliar culture and customs is what I have highlighted on his site.

Sidra my youngest one who loves to write stories, poetry, and draw. On her page is a tour of her books and stories.
